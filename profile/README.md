## About Affinity Photo
Affinity Photo is a professional-grade photo editing application designed to meet the demands of photographers, retouchers, and digital artists. It provides a comprehensive toolset that combines advanced photo manipulation capabilities with a modern, efficient workflow, offering a viable alternative to established industry standards. The application addresses the complete photographic workflow from RAW file development to final output, with specialized persona modes that optimize the interface for different tasks: Photo Persona for general editing, Liquify Persona for distortion effects, Develop Persona for RAW processing, Tone Mapping Persona for HDR work, and Export Persona for output optimization.

The application's strength lies in its combination of professional capabilities with performance optimization and modern workflow design. Built with a 64-bit architecture and GPU acceleration, it handles large files and complex edits with responsiveness. The non-destructive editing approach allows for experimentation and revision without compromising image quality, while features like focus stacking, panorama stitching, and HDR merging cater to specialized photographic techniques. For professionals and serious enthusiasts who require comprehensive editing tools without subscription commitments, Affinity Photo provides a robust, feature-complete solution that integrates well with its sibling applications, Designer and Publisher, for broader creative workflows.
